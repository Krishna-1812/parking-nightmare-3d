How to Play

1 · Drive

WASD/←↑↓→ to drive. Reverse steering works like a real car. Space handbrake, H horn.

2 · Park

Fit fully in the spot, within 8° of parallel, under 40cm from the curb — then hold still for 1.5s. Match the ghost!

3 · Don't Be Seen

Every bonk, honk and curb-mount feeds the Shame Meter. At 100% the crowd laughs and you fail. Drive smooth to calm it.
